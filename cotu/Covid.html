COVID-19

About COVID-19:

Infection with severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2, or SARS-CoV-2, causes coronavirus disease 2019 ( COVID-19 ). The virus that causes COVID-19 spreads easily among people.







    Symptoms of COVID-19

    Typical symptoms of COVID-19 include:

  • Fever or chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headache
  • New loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion or runny nose
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea

Causes of COVID-19:

  • COVID-19 virus spreads mainly from person to person among those in close contact.
  • Being in close contact with someone who has COVID-19, especially someone with symptoms
  • Being coughed or sneezed on by an infected person
  • Usinging drinking glasses or eating utensils used by infected person
  • Being near an infected person when in an indoor space with poor air flow
